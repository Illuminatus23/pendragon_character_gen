    Aging and Knighthood

    Knighthood requires the following:

    • Liege Loyalty of 15 (currently 0)
    • Skill of 10 in all starred skills above. (currently 0 of 7 skills are at 10)
    • Skill of 10 in any two additional non-combat skills (currently 0 of 2 skills are at 10)

    You will be aged 1 year for each point of Liege Loyalty gain.
    You will then gain 1 year for each 1d6 pts of skills.
    The first gains will occur automatically.
