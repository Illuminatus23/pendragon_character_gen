Personality Traits

We'll start with one of the most important topics for a knight, his vice and virtues.

Select a trait for which you are famous. Be careful to select one you can live with. Negative traits can be very detrimental in the world of Arthurian Knights. You will be able to adjust these later.

Religion also affects your starting trait levels as each religion values different virtues.

Note that bonuses and special abilities are awarded for knights that are Chivalrous (80 points in knightly virtues) and Religious (16+ in all Religious traits). You probably won't start with these bonuses but they can develop through play.

Statistics

You have 60 points to spend on your stats of Size, Dexterity, Strength, Constitution and Appearance. Because Size cannot be below 8 and the other stats cannot be below 5, these points have been spent for you already. Derived stats, under the line, will be computed automatically. For a Cymeric Knight, such as yourself, no stat can be above 18 with the exception of Constitution, which cannot rise above 21. In addition, Cymeric Knights get a +3 bonus to CON, which has been applied.
